10/10/2023:

today I am somber. feeling down. i am too sensitive and emotional for one lifetime, some days. wishing my emotions and relations with certain people weren't so complicated. i can rationalize how i should feel all day long, but my sappy heart does not care for logic and reasoning. i'm trying to distract myself from what's on my mind, but also trying to simply face it at face value. which approach i choose really wavers from moment to moment. but getting it out on a diary helps. i won't get too personal about it, since this is a public domain. i don't want to overshare, or make anyone's identy known. i'm missing someone, though! quite a bit. i cherish them very much, but sometimes it ain't so simple. In other news, I've got some music going. I've listened to three of tyler, the creator's albums today. re-listening, for the record, you scoundrels. I always try and dig his new releases whenever they pop up. right now, i think igor is my favorite. i adore the jazz tinges all over that album. some excellent chord work and modal solos. i'm lead to believe a lot of the playing is tyler himself. that man has come a loooong way as a musician. it's inspiring. gives me hope, and reminds me to practice. looking back on his album, bastard, it's simply jaw dropping, what he can do now. anyways, i think i'll get this blog post uploaded, and then try to play some acoustic guitar. distract myself a bit. ciao.

10/11/2023:

today was alright! i'm still a bit somber. it seems to come in seasons for me. usually it's not just not for a day. but i am feeling fortunate to have rekindled a friendship today. my emotions about the whole thing are a bit complex, and sometimes i let my emotions get the best of me. i certainly did last week. no matter what i feel though, they are above all else one of my best friends, and i don't want to let some of my emotional thoughts to overshadow that. there's no need to let my feelings bleed into the friendship like i did. a lot of what i feel is ultimately subjective, and i imagine certain feelings will pass eventually. we'll see i suppose. anyhow i felt flowers were a nice gesture to rekindle, and they were very understanding and gentle with me. everyone deserves a person like that in their lives. fortunate that the rich tapestry of my life is embroidered with that kind of human. in other news, i am busy as fuck at work. but work is over now, and it is past 9pm. i've had a calm evening. cleaned a bit. wrote a tune! i feel strongly about the instrumentation, but i still need to figure out what i'm going to sing. going for an alex g kinda thing. had candy for dinner. watching trash television (floribama shore). my life is much better than i like to think. it ain't so bad at all. adios!

10/12/2023:

to be determined. but i have a good feeling about it. - the day wasn't so bad! work has been crazy busy. we're down people on our team, and it was my turn to pick up some of the slack. double-triple the work for the same pay. fun. but..staying busy does keep me out of trouble. i have a tendency to get myself into trouble and make some pretty awful, self-destructive decisions. especially with idle time. usually it manifests into rampant drug and alcohol abuse. fortunately i have been clean for a bit now. but there's always tomorrow. it's a day by day ordeal. well, i do take kratom. that plant falls in a strange gray-area. is it a drug? well it does act on your opiod receptors and gives you the same withdrawl symptoms as heroin does. the last couple times i quit were some of the worst times of my life. buckets of sweat. didn't sleep for two weeks. literally. felt suicidal. but..it doesn't really get you high. just a slight and subtle euphoria and energy boost. it's a strange plant. anyhow, some people would argue that i am still off the rails, others not so much. it's up for debate with kratom. but..since i was so busy today, there was no time to really get myself into trouble. outside of work i played some guitar and bass. been watching ample amounts of trash-reality television. my favorite. ate a bit more than usual, which is good. i'm about 10 pounds underweight. but..i mostly just ate sugar cookies. i'm like an unsupervised child living out here on my own. it's peaceful though. i'm in in bed by 9:30-10:00. it's been cozy and peaceful today. certain people on my mind. oh, i also went on a date! her name is lauren. we walked around a park. had some great conversation. talked about spirituality, life, and books. very sweet woman. very attractive too, but, there was no spark. i don't intend on talking to her again. dating is such a strange thing in that way! i have met so many people recently for the first time, and then never again. only a couple people have really touched me in a way where i could daydream about being lovers. chemistry is a hard thing to come across. it's mostly pure chance. i suppose i won't find someone i have true chemistry with if i don't try though! i'll keep looking. ciao.

10/13/2023:

today has been a fine day. work was busy, but not unbearable. i cracked my laptop screen, so i had to go into the office for the first half of my day. got a new laptop. well..i reckon it's refurbished, used by a prior employee. don't think it was brand-new per se, but i don't mind. as long as it works. that's really my mantra for many things in life, particularly with all my musical gear. i just want functional. all those bells and whistles, or as the french say, "accoutrements", not my thing. i like simple and functional. and the laptop is that much. anyhow, went home on my lunch break, and then work was over. some days the time drags, other days it flies by. time is a strange concept. i reckon my life will feel both short and long when i'm facing my death and mortality one day. i keep envisioning myself dying at 45. i reckon 20 more years would make for a full life. all i really want is to be in true, deep love one more time. get a lil cult following for flaming peace. watch the cats live a full life. then i would be happy to go. death seems comforting. anyways, outside of work, i made a lil musical tune. forced myself. i don't really feel like being creative a lot of days. it was an alright tune. i always seem to get some love for my music on instagram, which is validating. aside from that, stu came by, and we went to a DIY art venue/garage called garage 129! it is a very lovely joint. i adored it.
