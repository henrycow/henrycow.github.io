10/24/2024:

good evening! it's 5:40 PM here. Getting chinese for dinner with my dad and brothers at 6:30 at lucky dragon on high st. my bday was yesterday (10/23) and so this is one of our handful of annual gatherings of the boys of the family. well i guess we're all men now. my bday was okay. i've been trying to quit sugar and binge eating again, so yesterday was pretty awful regarding cravings. it's been bad this last week or so, i last had sugar and overate candy on 10/17, so it's only the first week or so in. birthdays and holidays are always rough for me with cravings to go wild on junk food. i wish i were like the average person that is able to go wild on their bday with cake and what not, but if i do that, i won't stop. i'll binge sugar again more nights shortly after, and it will snowball. doesn't help that i have been off work all week so i'm not as distracted as i usually am. my thoughts have had ample time to wonder and it's been rough. either way, i am soldiering thru! day 8 off sugar (aside from natural sugar from fruit) and the worst of it will be over soon. i've checked the menu for lucky dragon online and got a couple healthy picks planned out. vietnamese spring rolls and chicken and broccoli w/ steamed rice. bringing my own hot sauce as well. i was gonna go for general tso's, but i had no clue that a lot of those sauces have so much sugar in them! it's wild how sugar is in so many foods. i've been doing well dieting otherwise. i had a really nasty 4-5 junk food binges in the last 3 weeks or so and wanted to get a lil calorie deficit going to undo those days. i'm shooting for roughly 1300-1500 calories a day and have been keeping up with it! i'm already a thin guy so some would view this as over the top, but i feel like i need to balance out all the extra sugar calories. i could deal with it being from real food, but not junk. i want to get back to where i was prior to those binges. fortunately i didn't set myself back too much at all. some people go off the rails daily for a month straight. ultimately though, i want to get to a place here soon where I don't think about food and what i'm eating. i'm hoping once the sugar cravings pass and i'm consistently eating healthy every day, and eating 1500-1900 cals, i'll settle into a good grind. i'm a skinny and very lazy couch potato so 1800-1900 cal is my TDEE. I just need to make sure that more often than not I am closer to 1900 cal. too long of a deficit and i set myself up for binge/restricting cycles, which i do struggle with. i'm thinking that i'll end the daily deficit at the end of october. anyways, so much for not oversharing, right? this is my personal diary at this point anyhow. if a couple people stumble across this i don't really care. i have reached a point recently where i have been very happy alone and don't very much care for what others think. it's been liberating. i used to care way too much about what people thought. anyhow, that's about all i wanna type tonight. just needed to write some of that out to help with my mental health. it's been a rough week but things always get better. i've never been in a funk permanently. this too shall pass. i'm looking forward to seeing my dad here soon. i love that guy. i played some world of warcraft and warcraft 3 today, and will probably game some more after dinner with my brother dante. we like to play pubg. keeping myself busy and distracted thru video games and youtube really helps with my wondering thoughts. well, that's a wrap. ciao!
