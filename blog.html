10/12/2023:

to be determined. but i have a good feeling about it. - the day wasn't so bad! work has been crazy busy. we're down people on our team, and it was my turn to pick up some of the slack. double-triple the work for the same pay. fun. but..staying busy does keep me out of trouble. i have a tendency to get myself into trouble and make some pretty awful, self-destructive decisions. especially with idle time. usually it manifests into rampant drug and alcohol abuse. fortunately i have been clean for a bit now. but there's always tomorrow. it's a day by day ordeal. well, i do take kratom. that plant falls in a strange gray-area. is it a drug?
